Some FAQs

Empowering Children Through Education Playful Mind

What is the prerequisities for this course?

Students need basic reading and writing skills, interest in learning, and discipline. No prior knowledge required. Parental support for practice and homework will help them achieve better results in this course.

What subject will I learn in school?

In school, you will learn subjects like English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and languages, along with Computer Science, Arts, Physical Education, and Moral Education to develop knowledge, skills, and values.

How long is a school day?

A school day usually lasts for about 6 to 7 hours, including classes, lunch break, and short breaks in between, depending on the grade and timetable of the school.

What will I learn in school?

In school, you will learn reading, writing, maths, science, social studies, languages, computer skills, arts, and moral values to build knowledge, skills, and good behaviour for life.
